QRPayHub (qrpayhub.com) is a free, privacy-first web application
for generating QR payment codes. All processing happens in the
user's browser – no payment data is ever sent to or stored on
any server.

Supported standards: GiroCode/EPC (SEPA), Swiss QR Code,
UPI (India), PIX (Brazil), PromptPay (Thailand),
QRIS (Indonesia), DuitNow (Malaysia), PayNow (Singapore),
FPS (Hong Kong), VietQR (Vietnam), QR Ph (Philippines),
CoDi (Mexico).

Languages: English, German.
Operator: Kaleb Jahnke, Germany.
Technology: Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vercel.
